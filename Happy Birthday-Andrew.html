Number 1 Song of August 20th

Dilemma-Nelly ft. Kelly Rowland

Dilemma by Nelly and Kelly Rowland was released on June 25th 2002, and would go on to become a number one hit for a few days the month after. Using a mixture of Pop and R&B, the song presents a man facing a dilemma, he has fallen in love with a woman, but she is already in a relationship.


Dilemma would go on to win a Grammy at the 45th Annual Grammy Awards. A few weeks after its debut, it stayed in the top 100 charts and held its position for 29 weeks.
