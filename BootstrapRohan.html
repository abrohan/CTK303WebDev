Disney Villainous

Disney Villainous is an asynchronous card game in which each player takes control over a villain of their choice in an attempt to complete their goals before the other player. By using certain actions, players can summon heroes to other players boards to impede their progress and gain an advantage. The game is complicated at first, but it is easy to understand the flow after a while. I play this all the time with my family due to the variety of characters, and the complexity of the characters adds a lot of skill while not being complicated that my parents don't understand what to do. I thought I would use this site to compile a list of all the many expansions(the Disney ones at least), maybe consider trying these out for yourself.

The Worst Takes All

Contains six characters:
Captain Hook: Defeat Peter Pan by utilizing his tricks and crew to gain the upper hand
Jafar: claim your seat as Sultan, while avoiding the pesky street rats that try to get in your way
Maleficent: Curse the realms and rule as the Mistress of all Evil
Ursula: Switch back and forth between your human form and the sea to decieve your way to ruling the sea
Queen of Hearts: Stack the cards in your favor as you try to win your game
Prince John: Become the richest in the realm as you collect all of your glorious taxes.

Wicked to the Core

Contains three additional characters:
Dr.Facilier: Add spells and tricks to your fortune deck, then let other players draw, and decide their fate.
Hades: Raise your mighty titans as you march on Olympus to claim your rightful throne.
Evil Queen: Concoct evil tricks to take out Snow White, and poison anyone who gets in your way.

Evil Comes Prepared

Contains three more characters:
Scar: Steal the throne of the Pridelands, and take out anyone who dares oppose you with your army of hyenas
Yzma: with the assistance of your henchman Kronk, you must find and take out Kuzko before he regains his human form.
Ratigan: devise a devious plan to take Buckingham Palace from under evryone's noses, just don't let that foolish detective Basil get in your way.

Perfectly Wretched

Contains an additional three characters:
Pete: Clear four tasks from your board using your trusty steamboat to guide you
Mother Gothel: Win over the trust of Rapunzel through various tricks, or lose your immortality forever.
Cruella Deville: Collect 101 dalmations to complete your collection, if your incompetent hanchmen can grab them.

Despicable Plots

Three more characters added:
Horned King: Unleash the horrors of the Black Cauldron on the land, and forge your unstoppable army to crush your enemies.
Gaston: Prove that no one in the land is a great as you, and that not horrific beast shall stand in your path.
Lady Tremaine: One of your daughters must marry the prince tonight, and you must make sure it won't be Cinderella.

Bigger and Badder

Another three characters added:
Madam Mim: use various animal transformations to duel Merlin and prove who is the greatest in a wizard duel.
Syndrome: Bide your time and upgrade your Omnidroid, and prove that supers aren't what they are cracked out to be.
Lotso: Decrease the power of any toy that tries to escape, and prove that you are the master of Sunnyside.

Filled with Fright

Contains one additional character:
Oogie Boogie: Roll dice and lure Jack Skellington into a trap to become the true King of Halloween

Marvel Villainous

A new way to play villainous seperate from the Disney version of the game, complete with an expansion as well.

Star Wars Villainous

Another sub-division of Villainous, with new special abilities to shake up battle, and vehicle cards to add more space to your board.
