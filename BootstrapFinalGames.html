Cyberpaw

The First game that I made in collaboration with the Illinois State University Game Dev Club. CyberPaw was a futuristic 2d sidescroller run and gun game where you play as a fox in an attempt to save the future from its terrible ruler. You would use various weapons to grant buffs to get an edge over the enemy. I worked as a level designer for this one.

Fear of Flora

Fear of Flora was another game I made in the Game Dev Club. It was a top down tower defense game where you used edlritch horror plants to fight off angry townspeople from destroying your land. I worked as a level designer where I decided the layout of the background tilemap, and some of the wave layouts.

Gobbotron Neon Sins

Gobbotron Neon Sins was the third game I made in the club. Similar to Cyberpaw, it was a 2d sidescoller run and gun game.

UI+UX

Game I made in UI+UX with another person.

Woodland Warfare

Woodland Warfare was a game I made Sophomore year, where I worked in a group with only one other person. It was a competitive survival game where players must fight for resources to survive the four seasons as a woodland critter. I worked on various art, and the entire ruleset for the game.

Underwater Game

A 2d sidecroller shooter that I made in one of my classes. This was made with assets from the Unity store, where you must control a fish and a starfish simultaniously to fight off various enemies in order to achieve the highest score possible.
